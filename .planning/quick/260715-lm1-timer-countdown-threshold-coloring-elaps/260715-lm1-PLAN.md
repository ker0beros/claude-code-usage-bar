<objective>
Give the reset-timer countdowns (`⏰{reset_time}`) their OWN threshold-based color driven by elapsed% of each window, decoupled from the usage bar's color. Today the timer text just inherits the bar's usage-severity color (`color_5h` / `color_7d`). This makes the timer color a separate time-driven signal on fixed 65/85 cutoffs, with the 5h mapping FLIPPED (short countdown = fresh quota imminent = green) and the 7d mapping NORMAL (short countdown = window running out = red).

Purpose: A short 5h countdown is GOOD and should read green; a short 7d countdown is BAD and should read red — a signal the current bar-inherited coloring cannot express.
Output: One shared helper reused by all 3 styles + preview; the bar fill/label and the 5h ✨/⚡/🎉 emoji stay unchanged.
</objective>
