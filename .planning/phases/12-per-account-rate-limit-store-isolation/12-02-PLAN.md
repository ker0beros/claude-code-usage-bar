<objective>
Make predict.py's store keying per-session-account-aware, reusing account.py's Phase 11
resolver, via the LOCKED sentinel-based optional-parameter design (12-CONTEXT.md
"predict.py session-context threading"). Add a keying-specific `.claude.json` locator that
enforces R5b (a named dir without its own `.claude.json` never borrows `$HOME/.claude.json`),
a dedicated memoized reader, and thread an `account_uuid` param through every store consumer —
including the two RESEARCH landmines (`regime_changed_at` inside `projection()`, and
`_projection_result_key`).

Purpose: R1/R2/R4/R5 — the shared `rate_latest.<uuid>.json` / `rate_projection.<uuid>.json`
bucketing collapses concurrent accounts onto one bucket because `account_id()` hardcodes
`~/.claude.json`. Keying each store to the session's own resolved uuid removes the collision
while preserving byte-identical legacy behavior for unresolvable sessions.

Output: a per-session-aware predict.py; the 11 unit/landmine/prohibition tests from 12-01 turn
GREEN (the e2e regression stays RED until core.py wiring in 12-03).
</objective>
